Times and Facilities

  • Practice times area scheduled throughout the week.
  • Practices are held at Glencliff HS (managed by the YMCA of Middle TN) and/or Tennessee State University - Gentry Complex Pool.
  • TSU Pool has one 1 meter and one 3 meter board. Glencliff Pool has two 1 meter boards.
    • Tuesday & Thursday 6-8pm at Glencliff HS
    • Monday & Wednesday 4-6pm at TSU Pool
    • First half hour is dryland training & 1.5 hours on the diving boards
  • Other times may also be arranged (Ex. Meets, Fridays & Weekends).
