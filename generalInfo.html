Middle TN Diving-General Information

General Information

  • Open to boys and girls age 10 and up. (Must be comfortable in deep water).
  • Available for beginner lessons through competitive team on both 1 meter (low board) and 3 meter (high board).
  • AAU & USA Diving competition is also available, with competitions against other regional and national competitors.
  • High school & middle school programs representing all local schools.

Diving Groups

  • Bronze Group: beginners and younger athletes.
  • Siver Group: divers with some experience (All 5 diving groups represented).
  • Gold Group: divers with all competitive groups, hich school competition ready (11 dive list).

Please join the Middle TN Diving Program in the GroupMe phone app.
